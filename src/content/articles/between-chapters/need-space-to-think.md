## Space is a condition you can design

Choose a duration small enough to protect. Define the one subject you will consider and the inputs you will suspend. Change location if useful, but do not make travel a prerequisite.

Spend the first third decompressing, the second observing and writing, and the final third choosing what happens next. Keep the outcome proportionate: one question clarified, one boundary named, one experiment chosen.
