## Do not demand a map too soon

Feeling lost can describe many ordinary and difficult transitions. It is not a diagnosis. Before searching for a complete new direction, create a smaller form of orientation.

What is no longer true? What still feels solid? Which decisions are urgent, and which merely feel loud? What would make the next week more honest?

Take a walk without input, talk with someone trustworthy, or protect an afternoon for writing. If distress is persistent, severe, or affects safety and daily functioning, seek support from a qualified professional or local service.
