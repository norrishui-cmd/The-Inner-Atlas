## Give absence a purpose

Removing a screen creates space; a retreat gives that space a shape. Begin with an intention, choose a defined duration, and prepare two or three offline practices: walking, writing, cooking, reading, or sitting outside.

Notice the moments you reach automatically. Write down what you wanted from the device—novelty, reassurance, escape, connection—without judging it.

Close by choosing one durable boundary. Use the [retreat builder](/build-your-retreat/) for a plan matched to your time.
