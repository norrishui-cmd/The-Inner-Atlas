## Set the boundary before Friday

Name what is off-limits, what remains functional, and when you will return. Download essential information, tell close contacts, and choose one emergency channel.

On Saturday, protect the morning from input and do one physical, place-based activity. Allow one short practical check-in if needed. On Sunday, notice which apps you did not miss and restore notifications selectively.

Success is not zero screen minutes. It is a more deliberate relationship with attention.
