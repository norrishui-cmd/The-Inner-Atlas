## Keep utility, reduce drift

Travel often makes a phone necessary. Separate utility from drift: download maps, screenshot bookings, pin emergency information, and move social and work apps off the home screen.

Choose specific photo moments rather than documenting continuously. Use airplane mode during walks and meals. Let navigation return only when needed.

Digital distance should never undermine safety. Keep communication available when traveling alone, in unfamiliar terrain, or where conditions can change.
