## Place is not a cure

The places in Dunhuang Within are not ranked by mystical power or sold as guarantees of change. We explore how geography, history, craft, and cultural encounter can widen perspective when approached with attention.

Our first place is [Dunhuang](/places/dunhuang/): an oasis on historic Silk Roads, at the edge of desert landscapes and home to the Mogao Caves.

Every place guide prioritizes institutional and scholarly sources, separates documented history from interpretation, and asks visitors to respect conservation, living communities, and access rules.
