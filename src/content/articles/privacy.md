## Your information

The Personal Retreat Builder runs in your browser. Its answers are not sent to our server by default. If analytics is configured, aggregate interaction events may be recorded without the text of journal responses.

Email addresses are submitted only when you choose an email action and a newsletter provider has been connected. We do not sell personal information. This MVP contains no user accounts.

Contact details and provider-specific retention terms will be added before production email collection is enabled.
