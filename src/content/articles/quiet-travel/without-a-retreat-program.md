## Structure without a program

You do not need a retreat center to protect attention. Choose a stable base, reduce transit, set a daily phone window, and alternate light structure with true blank time.

A useful rhythm is simple: quiet morning, one place-based activity, unhurried meal, empty hour, short reflection. Keep plans visible on paper so your phone does not become the doorway back to everything else.
