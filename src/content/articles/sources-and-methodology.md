## How we research

For history and cultural heritage, we prioritize UNESCO, site stewards such as the Dunhuang Academy, museums, libraries, and peer-reviewed scholarship. Travel conditions should always be confirmed with current official sources before a trip.

Search trend data informs internal editorial priorities but is not presented as absolute search volume. We do not publish synthetic statistics as facts.

Reflective guidance is educational and non-clinical. It is designed to create structure, not to diagnose or promise outcomes.
