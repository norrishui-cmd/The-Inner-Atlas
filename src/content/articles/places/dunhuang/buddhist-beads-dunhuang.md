## A bead is not automatically a mala

A Buddhist mala or prayer rosary belongs to a living religious practice. A fashion bracelet may borrow bead forms without serving that function. A Dunhuang-inspired object can reference a mural, color, or material while remaining a contemporary design. The respectful choice is to name which one you are buying.

## Questions to ask before buying

Ask whether the maker identifies the source image, describes the materials, avoids fake relic language, and explains the intended use. Be cautious with claims of instant protection, healing, wealth, or spiritual rank. Cultural respect begins with accurate naming.
