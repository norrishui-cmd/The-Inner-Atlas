## Begin with the intention, not the promise

Ask what quality you want to practice: courage, clarity, compassion, steadiness, renewal or direction. This gives the bracelet a role in your daily behavior without requiring a claim that the stone changes health, luck or external events.

## Five questions before choosing

1. **What intention does it remind me to practice?**
2. **Will its color and weight invite regular attention?**
3. **Is the stone identified accurately?**
4. **Can the seller explain sourcing, treatment and construction?**
5. **Are the claims honest and proportionate?**

## Connecting crystal color with a Dunhuang path

- Earth and warm mineral tones can accompany the **Desert Crossing** and courage.
- Dark or translucent stones can accompany the **Hidden Cave** and reflection.
- Green and water tones can accompany the **Oasis Return** and renewal.
- Layered or directional forms can accompany the **Silk Road Path** and purposeful movement.

These are Dunhuang Within design associations, not ancient Dunhuang crystal doctrines.

## A daily bracelet practice

Choose one phrase linked to your intention. Each time the bracelet catches your attention, take one unforced breath and repeat the phrase silently. The bracelet becomes an anchor because of the relationship you build with it.
