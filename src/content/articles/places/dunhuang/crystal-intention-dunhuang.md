## Start with material, then intention

Dunhuang colors were made from real mineral and organic materials, shaped by craft and time. A modern crystal object can echo that material attention, but a stone does not have a proven ability to heal, protect, or change fate.

Choose a stone by touch, color, durability, and the intention you want to remember. “Clarity,” “patience,” or “return” are useful personal prompts; they are not properties guaranteed by a mineral.
