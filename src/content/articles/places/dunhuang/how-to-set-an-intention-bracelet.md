## Keep the sentence small

An effective intention is specific enough to guide a choice. Try "I will take one clear next step" rather than a broad promise that everything will change. The bracelet's role is simply to bring the sentence back to your attention.

## Pair it with an action

When you touch the bracelet, take one slow breath, name the next useful action, and do it if you can. The value is in the repeated relationship between attention and behavior—not in a claim that beads or stones alter external events.

## Change it when the season changes

An intention can last a day, a month or a difficult transition. When it no longer feels alive, choose another.
