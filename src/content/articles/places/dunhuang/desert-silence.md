## Silence is never empty

Around Dunhuang, wind, distance, temperature, and exposure shape the experience of quiet. Desert silence is not a supernatural treatment. It can, however, reduce familiar visual and auditory demands and invite careful attention.

Go with realistic expectations and local guidance. Conditions can be severe; carry water, sun protection, and appropriate layers, and respect restricted or fragile areas.

After returning, write what the scale changed in your perception—without asking the landscape to explain your life.
