## “Cleansing” can mean two different things

Physical cleaning removes skin oils, dust and residue. Spiritual cleansing is a personal or traditional ritual intended to reset one's relationship with an object. The second should not be presented as a scientifically measured removal of negative energy.

## Safe physical care

First identify every stone, metal, coating, elastic and cord. Some materials can be damaged by water, salt, sunlight, heat, abrasives or household chemicals. Follow product-specific care instructions. When unsure, use a soft dry cloth and ask the maker before soaking anything.

## A simple intention reset

Place the bracelet on a clean surface. Take three ordinary breaths. Recall why you chose it, then name one action that would express that intention today. No smoke, salt, moonlight or spoken formula is required unless it is meaningful within your own informed practice.

## When to stop wearing it

Repair or retire a bracelet when cord, clasp or setting becomes unsafe. Do not interpret normal breakage as proof of absorbed danger or a predicted event.
