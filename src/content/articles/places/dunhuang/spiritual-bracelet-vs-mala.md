## The difference in one sentence

A spiritual or intention bracelet is a broad contemporary category, while a mala is traditionally connected to counting recitations, mantras or prayers within religious practice. Appearance alone does not make the two interchangeable.

## Intention bracelet

An intention bracelet may use stone, metal, cord or symbolic forms. Its purpose is personally defined: it can remind the wearer of courage, compassion, clarity or another chosen quality. There is no universal bead count or required ritual.

## Crystal bracelet

A crystal bracelet is defined primarily by material. Sellers may associate stones with metaphysical qualities, but those associations should not be confused with medical evidence. At Dunhuang Within, stone, color and touch support attention rather than a promise of cure.

## Buddhist mala

A mala belongs to a living field of Buddhist and other South Asian religious practices. Bead counts and use vary by tradition. If an object is sold as a mala, its construction and description should reflect that context accurately rather than using the name only to make generic jewelry appear sacred.

## Which should you choose?

Choose a mala when you understand and intend to use it within an appropriate practice. Choose an intention bracelet when you want an accessible daily reminder without claiming a formal religious function. In both cases, ask about material, construction, source and meaning.
