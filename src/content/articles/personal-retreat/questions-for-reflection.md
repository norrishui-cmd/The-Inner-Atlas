## Questions that leave room

- What is asking to end, even if nothing is wrong?
- Which part of your life still fits—and which part only looks familiar?
- What becomes audible when nobody needs an answer from you?
- What are you optimizing that no longer matters?
- Which expectation did you inherit without choosing?
- What would enough look like for the next season?
- What can remain unresolved for now?
- What is one next step small enough to be honest?

Choose one or two. A retreat is not improved by answering everything.
