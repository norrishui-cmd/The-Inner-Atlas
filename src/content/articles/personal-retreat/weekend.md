## Friday: arrive

Let the first evening be a transition rather than a productivity session. Eat, unpack, place the phone away, and write what can wait until Sunday.

## Saturday: leave room

Use one morning reflection, one long walk, and one empty afternoon block. Do not crowd the schedule with wellness tasks. Rest is allowed to be the content.

## Sunday: return deliberately

Review what became clearer and what did not. Choose one boundary to carry home. Re-enter messages in a defined window rather than opening every channel at once.
